Wow! Meal Planning in Your Daily Life,
Let's add balance and diversity into every bite

Our app combines scheduling meals, making grocery lists and storing recipes in one area. It helps users save time on planning meals by allowing users to randomly generate diverse meals that suit their needs and tastes. Users can use tags to further specify, for example, what cuisines and ingredients they would like to be generated.
